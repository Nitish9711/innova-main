About Us
INNOVA, the techno-management fest of Delhi Technological University (formerly known as Delhi College of Engineering) was founded in 1994. The largest fest of its kind in North India, INNOVA has carved out its name and a niche for itself in the past 25 years. Started as a collaboration between the Department of Mechanical and Production Engineering and the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering,INNOVA has always aimed to encourage skill development, talent and creativity across all branches and fileds. Our philosophy

At Innova we believe in EDUCATE, INNOVATE and LEAD It is our belief that everyone on this planet is an innovator. We feel responsible towards the innovator in all of us and strive to provide a platform to everyone to come together to share, learn and grow as a community. We believe in making a better world by making better leaders and creating better technology fora better tomorrow. We trust innovation and so we trust Innova.
